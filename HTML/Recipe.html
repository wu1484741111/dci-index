Ingrediant:

    1. Main Base
      • 1 piece of rice noodle,
    2. Flesh
      • Shredded pork 100g,
      • 1 tbsp shrimp,
    3. Vegetable
      • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms,
      • 2 cilantro plants,
      • 3 slices of cabbage,
    4. Sauce
      • soy sauce,
      • Rice wine,
    5. Spice
      • White pepper,
      • Garlic,
      • 3 pieces of red onion,
      • 1 tbsp. minced garlic
Original Video


Steps:

  1. Add oil and salt to the boiling water, then add the rice noodles to the pot and cook for about 1 minute.

  2. After 1 minute, salvage the rice noodles into a bowl, cover with a lid to stew, to increase the Q-string texture

  3. Add garlic, red onion and oil to the frying pan and sauté until browned.

  4. Stir-fry the mushrooms in the original pan, then add 1 spoon of kaiyang and stir-fry, then add the meat (if you use pancetta, you don't need to add more oil) and cabbage.

  5. Drizzle soy sauce along the side of the hot pot and stir-fry, then add rice wine and simmer in KAIYANG water and mushroom water to increase the sweetness of cabbage, then sprinkle with white pepper.

  6. #Reminder 1：When frying rice noodles, don't add too much water, but fry until dry.

  7. #Reminder 2: Slightly cut the rice noodles just put braised (use scissors), easy to fry

  8. Add the rice noodles to the pot and stir-fry with the ingredients, sprinkle with cilantro

  9. Use chopsticks to spread the rice noodles to increase the elasticity of the taste, pour in the red onion oil before starting the pot, turn off the heat and mix well.
